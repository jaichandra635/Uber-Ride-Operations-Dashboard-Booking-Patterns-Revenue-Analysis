# Uber-Ride-Operations-Dashboard-Booking-Patterns-Revenue-Analysis
Comprehensive analysis of Uber's 2024 operations: 148K+ bookings across vehicle types. Uncovers insights on booking trends, revenue optimization, cancellation patterns, and customer metrics

# Business Problem Statement

## Executive Summary

Uber's ride-sharing platform processes over 148,000 bookings annually across multiple vehicle types. However, comprehensive operational visibility into booking patterns, revenue drivers, and service delivery metrics remains limited. This analysis is conducted to unlock actionable insights from transactional data and identify optimization opportunities across key operational dimensions.

## Objectives

**Primary Goals:**
- Understand ride booking patterns and demand fluctuations across time periods and vehicle types
- Identify revenue optimization opportunities and stream contributors
- Analyze cancellation behaviors and their impact on platform efficiency
- Evaluate customer satisfaction metrics and service quality indicators
- Detect operational inefficiencies and bottlenecks impacting profitability

**Secondary Goals:**
- Establish baseline KPIs for ongoing performance monitoring
- Enable data-driven decision-making for resource allocation
- Provide strategic recommendations for platform enhancement

## Current State Analysis

The organization currently lacks:
- Comprehensive understanding of booking dynamics and peak demand periods
- Clear visibility into revenue distribution across vehicle categories
- Structured analysis of cancellation root causes and financial impact
- Customer satisfaction trend analysis and retention drivers
- Performance benchmarking against operational targets

## Expected Deliverables

- Exploratory data analysis revealing key business drivers
- Customer segmentation and booking pattern insights
- Revenue impact analysis of cancellations and service metrics
- Actionable recommendations for operational efficiency
- Dashboard-ready visualizations for stakeholder communication
